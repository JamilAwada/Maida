About

Maida - مائدة

is a user-procured food delivery platform where users can post their own dishes for sale and/ or order other users’ dishes. The website provides a platform for a wide niche of users that previously relied on suboptimal methods of communicating and looking for food products. It is an easy-to-use method to both showcase food items for sale as well as have homemade meals delivered while supporting local small businesses and individuals. Nonetheless, it is also deacreases the chance of getting food waste where instead of throwing away unplanned additional portions of meals, the chef can simply create an account, post the dish and sell it to whomever may want to buy it. Maida's developers are Hanine Al Khatib, Jamil Awada and Mohammed Rissal Hedna.
